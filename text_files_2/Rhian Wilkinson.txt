She captured an Olympic bronze medal at London 2012 after Canada defeated France 1\u20130 on August 9, 2012, in Coventry, England. She contributed three assists in the first two games for Canada at the 2014 Cyprus Cup. Wilkinson announced her retirement from international football on January 13, 2017.\n\n\n== Coaching career ==\nWilkinson was a volunteer assistant coach at her alma mater Tennessee Volunteers.
