As part of the organizing committee of the DFB for the 2006 FIFA World Cup, Sandrock was Tournament Director from 2003 to 2006. After that, he was Managing Director of FC Red Bull Salzburg from 2006 to 2008, with whom he won the Austrian football championship in 2007. With FIFA, Sandrock served as general coordinator at the FIFA Club World Cups in 2006 and 2007, as well as, at the 2009 FIFA Confederations Cup and the 2010 FIFA World Cup. In 2008, he also organized for FIFA the first home game of the Palestine national football team.In 2008 he became DFB director for league operations, talent promotion, the coaching system and the junior national teams. On March 2, 2012, Sandrock was elected Secretary General of the DFB. He was also elected UEFA integrations officer in 2012. In February 2016, Sandrock resigned as DFB general secretary and was succeeded by Friedrich Curtius.
